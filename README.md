# Sales_Performance_Analysis
This repo contains a sales analysis project done in Excel and SQL.

⭐ SQL SALES DATA ANALYSIS PROJECT
📊 Sales Performance Analysis (Jan – June)

✅ 1. Project Title
Sales Performance Analysis Using SQL and Excel (First Half of the Year)

✅ 2. Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing company sales performance for the first half of the year (January to June) using SQL & Excel.
The goal of the analysis is to uncover insights related to:
•	Monthly revenue trends
•	Product performance
•	Regional sales distribution
•	High-value transactions
•	Revenue efficiency
The analysis helps business stakeholders make informed strategic decisions regarding product focus, regional expansion, and revenue optimization.

✅ 3. Business Problem
The Head of Sales and Strategy Department requested a performance review of company sales data for the first half of the year.
The objective was to:
•	Identify the best performing products
•	Understand revenue trends across months
•	Evaluate regional performance
•	Detect high-revenue transactions
•	Measure product revenue efficiency
These insights will support future sales planning and operational improvements.

✅ 4. Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional sales records with the following fields:
Column:	Description
Month:	Month of transaction (January – June)
Region:	Sales region (e.g., Ibadan, Lagos, Abuja)
Product:	Product sold (Zobo, Tigernut Drink, Popcorn, Kilishi)
Units_Sold:	Quantity of product sold
Revenue	Total: revenue generated (₦)

✅ 5. Tools & Technologies Used
•	SQL (MySQL / PostgreSQL)
•	SQL Workbench / pgAdmin
•	CSV Dataset
•	Data Aggregation Functions
•	Grouping & Filtering Techniques

✅ 6. Project Objectives
This analysis aimed to:
•	Retrieve sales records within a defined time period
•	Calculate revenue performance across months
•	Evaluate product demand using units sold
•	Identify highest revenue generating products
•	Determine weakest performing regions
•	Measure revenue efficiency per product
•	Detect top revenue transactions

✅ 7. Methodology
The project followed a structured data analysis workflow:
⭐ Step 1 — Data Preparation
A sales table was created and the dataset was imported into the SQL database.
⭐ Step 2 — Data Exploration
Basic queries were used to understand:
•	Data distribution
•	Revenue ranges
•	Transaction patterns
⭐ Step 3 — Aggregation Analysis
SQL aggregation functions such as:
•	SUM()
•	AVG()
•	GROUP BY
•	ORDER BY
•	LIMIT
were used to generate insights.
⭐ Step 4 — Performance Ranking
Products and regions were ranked based on:
•	Total revenue
•	Units sold
•	Revenue efficiency

✅ 8. Key SQL Analysis Performed
The following business questions were answered using SQL:
•	Total revenue generated per month
•	Total units sold per product
•	Highest revenue generating product
•	Region with lowest sales performance
•	Top 3 products across all regions
•	Average monthly revenue per region
•	High value transactions above ₦50,000
•	Revenue efficiency per product

✅ 9. Key Insights & Findings
📊 Revenue Trend
Revenue varied across months with peak performance observed during high-demand periods.
🥇 Top Performing Product
One product - Kilishi; consistently generated the highest total revenue of 12819, indicating strong market demand.
🌍 Regional Performance
Abuja region significantly outperformed others in total revenue generation, suggesting differences in market penetration or customer demand.
📉 Weakest Region
The lowest revenue region - Lagos, may require improved marketing efforts or sales strategies.
💰 High-Value Transactions
Several transactions exceeded ₦50,000, indicating bulk purchases or premium pricing opportunities.
⚡ Revenue Efficiency
Product such as Kilishi with higher average revenue per unit demonstrate better profitability potential.

✅ 10. Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis:
•	Increase inventory and marketing for top-performing products
•	Investigate low-revenue regions for operational improvement
•	Encourage bulk purchase promotions to increase high-value transactions
•	Optimize pricing strategies for high revenue-per-unit products
•	Monitor monthly revenue patterns for better forecasting

This SQL sales analysis project demonstrates the ability to:

•	Write optimized SQL aggregation queries
•	Extract actionable insights from transactional datasets
•	Translate data findings into business recommendations
The project reflects real-world data analyst responsibilities and showcases foundational skills in SQL-based performance analysis.
